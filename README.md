# Geopolitical Risk Intelligence Platform

## Overview

A subscription SaaS platform that combines open-source intelligence (OSINT), think-tank research, financial flow data, and economic modeling to generate real-time geopolitical risk scores and scenario simulations for decision-makers.

The platform aggregates policy signals, capital movements, and global events to help institutional investors, governments, think tanks, and defense contractors evaluate geopolitical risk and model potential outcomes such as sanctions, conflicts, regulatory shifts, and supply-chain disruptions.

By bridging policy analysis with financial forecasting, the system functions as a decision-support engine for asset allocation, strategic planning, and war-gaming future geopolitical scenarios.

## Core Value Proposition

- Convert fragmented geopolitical, policy, and financial data into a unified risk intelligence layer.
- Provide continuously updated country, sector, corridor, and event-level risk scores.
- Simulate second-order effects across markets, trade routes, supply chains, and regulatory environments.
- Support high-stakes decision-making with both analyst-facing research workflows and executive-facing dashboards.

## Primary Users

- Institutional investors
- Sovereign entities and government agencies
- Think tanks and policy analysts
- Defense contractors
- Strategic advisory and risk teams

## Core Product Modules

### 1. Intelligence Ingestion

- OSINT collection from news, public filings, government statements, sanctions lists, and social channels
- Structured ingestion of think-tank and policy research
- Financial flow data pipelines for capital movement, trade exposure, and market signals
- Event normalization and entity resolution across countries, firms, sectors, and policymakers

### 2. Risk Scoring Engine

- Real-time geopolitical risk scoring by geography, sector, theme, and issuer
- Signal weighting across political, military, regulatory, and economic indicators
- Confidence scoring based on source quality, corroboration, and recency
- Alerting on material score changes and threshold breaches

### 3. Scenario Simulation

- Forward-looking scenario generation for sanctions, conflicts, policy changes, and supply-chain shocks
- Economic and market impact modeling across commodities, FX, rates, and equities
- Counterfactual analysis with probability-weighted outcomes
- War-gaming tools for strategic and operational planning

### 4. Decision Support Interface

- Dashboard for monitoring live risk posture
- Research workspace for analyst narratives and evidence review
- Portfolio and exposure views for asset allocation decisions
- API access for downstream models, internal systems, and client reporting

## Example Use Cases

- Assess the likely market and supply-chain impact of sanctions on a strategic region.
- Model the effect of a regulatory shift on defense, energy, or semiconductor exposures.
- Monitor escalation risk around elections, border disputes, or military posturing.
- Stress test investment portfolios against geopolitical shock scenarios.
- Support public-sector planning with scenario-based strategic analysis.

## MVP Scope

- Ingest a limited set of public data sources and policy feeds
- Build entity resolution for countries, sectors, and major public companies
- Generate baseline geopolitical risk scores with analyst-configurable weights
- Provide scenario templates for sanctions, conflict escalation, and trade disruption
- Deliver results through a web dashboard and API

## Positioning

This platform sits between traditional geopolitical advisory firms, static research products, and quantitative market intelligence tools. Its differentiator is combining policy interpretation, financial flow analysis, and simulation tooling in one operating system for geopolitical decision-making.
